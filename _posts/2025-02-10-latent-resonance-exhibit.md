title: "Latent Resonance shown at CINETic Research Day"

description: Presented an interactive audiovisual installation exploring latent space navigation through physical gesture.
---

*Latent Resonance* was shown as a work-in-progress at the CINETic Research Day in Bucharest. Visitors could navigate a GAN latent space in real time using hand gestures captured via a depth camera, with audio synthesis driven by the same trajectory.
